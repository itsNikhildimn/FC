Front-End Developer

We are looking for a Front-End Developer for building engaging and interactive user interfaces that bring websites and web applications to life. The successful candidate will be responsible for translating design concepts into responsive and functional websites and web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain web-based user interfaces that adhere to design specifications and coding best practices.

  • Collaborate with designers, back-end developers, and project managers to ensure timely delivery of high-quality products.

  • Optimize website performance by identifying and resolving technical issues related to front-end development.

  • Stay up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies in web development, and apply them to improve the user experience and functionality of our products.

  • Conduct thorough testing and debugging to ensure smooth and error-free user experiences across different browsers and devices.

  • Write clean, well-documented, and reusable code that is easy to maintain and scale.

  • Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback to other team members.

  • Communicate effectively with project stakeholders to ensure that project requirements and timelines are met.

Requirments:

  • Proven experience as a front-end developer, with a strong portfolio of web development projects.

  • Proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as front-end frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue.js.

  • Solid understanding of cross-browser compatibility issues and responsive web design principles.

  • Experience with version control systems such as Git.

  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills, with the ability to troubleshoot complex issues.

  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills, with the ability to work effectively in a collaborative environment.

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field is preferred, but not required.
